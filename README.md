LastMile Service is a trust-based digital marketplace designed to connect rural and semi-urban households with verified local technicians and appliance brands. The platform aims to make after-sales service more reliable, affordable, and accessible in underserved regions of India.

---

## üìñ Project Overview

In many rural areas, people face difficulties in accessing quality appliance repair services. They often depend on informal technicians or travel long distances to authorized service centers. This project addresses these challenges by providing a centralized digital platform for booking, tracking, and reviewing repair services.

LastMile Service connects three main stakeholders:
- Consumers
- Local Technicians
- Appliance Brands
